Introducing our new pizza concept

Order from select True Food Kitchen locations

Spring/Summer Menu 2022

Welcome to Good Slice Pizza by True Food Kitchen, a deliciously fun pizza concept that invites you to try the good slice and live the good life. We’re offering handmade pizzas, sandwiches, entrees, salads, desserts, and refreshing beverages available for convenient delivery. All of our pizzas can be ordered on gluten-free crust. Good Slice is available for delivery only in select True Food Kitchen locations, including Tempe, AZ; San Jose, CA; Reston, VA; Fairfax, VA, and Bethesda, MD.
