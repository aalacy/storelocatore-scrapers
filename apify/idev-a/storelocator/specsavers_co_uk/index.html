    Store services available

    Our Yardley store offers eye health and hearing services.

    Eye health services

    What to expect: Specsavers provide a number of audiology services, including hearing tests, earwax removal, hearing aid fitting and replacement.

    Find out more

    What to expect: Your eyes can actually tell us a lot about your general health, even if they dont feel any different. Thats why its so important to have your eyes tested regularly.

    Find out more

    What to expect: Childrens eye tests are important to make sure there arent any issues with their vision that could affect their school years.

    Find out more

    What to expect: Specsavers offer lots of different types of contact lenses to suit your lifestyle and prescription. Book an appointment at your local store to chat about contact lenses in more detail.

    Find out more

    What to expect: If youre diabetic, you should already be part of a screening programme. In certain parts of the country, we provide this regular screening service on behalf of the NHS. Speak to your local Specsavers optometrist if you have any concerns.

    What to expect: Specsavers offer eye tests on behalf of the DVLA. Book an appointment with your local Specsavers store.

    What to expect: Our team of home visiting opticians carry out home-friendly eye tests for those who cannot get to one of our stores unaccompanied. Find out if you, or someone you know, is eligible for a home eye test with Specsavers.

    Find out more

    What to expect: Minor eye conditions include things like watery eye, dry eye, red eye and blepharitis. Speak to your local Specsavers store about how we can help.

    What to expect: Your eyes can tell you a lot about your general health as well as your health, which is why its so important to have them checked regularly.

    What to expect: An optical coherence tomography scan (OCT) helps us to view the health of your eyes in greater detail, by allowing us to see whats going on beneath the surface of the eye.

    Find out more

    What to expect: Sometimes its not possible to visit us in store. Our RemoteCare service provides sight or hearing advice via phone or video consultation, even if youre not a current customer.

    Hearing services

    What to expect: Our audiologists will test your hearing to find out how well you can hear and whether youd benefit from hearing aids.

    Find out more
